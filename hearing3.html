Councilmember Mary Cheh, Chair of the Committee on Transportation and the Environment, announces a public oversight roundtable of the Committee on Transportation and the Environment on Parking in the Distric. The public oversight roundtable will take place at 11:00 a.m. on Wednesday, January 29, 2014 in Room 500 of the John A. Wilson Building.

The purpose of the oversight roundtable is to to receive testimony on-street parking in the District and the District Department of Transportation's parking program and Parking Action Agenda. Topics will include residential parking, visitor parking, commercial parking, performance parking, and accessible parking..

Those who wish to testify are asked to telephone the Committee on Transportation and the Environment, at (202) 724-8062, or e-mail Aukima Benjamin, staff assistant to the Committee on Transportation and the Environment, at abenjamin@dccouncil.us, and furnish their name, address, telephone number, and organizational affiliation, if any, by the close of business on Tuesday, January 28, 2014. Persons wishing to testify are encouraged, but not required, to submit 10 copies of written testimony. If submitted by the close of business on Tuesday, January 28, 2014, the testimony will be distributed to Councilmembers before the hearing. Witnesses should limit their testimony to three minutes; less time will be allowed if there are a large number of witnesses.

If you are unable to testify at the hearing, written statements are encouraged and will be made a part of the official record. Copies of written statements should be submitted to the Committee on Transportation and the Environment, Council of the District of Columbia, 1350 Pennsylvania Avenue, N.W., Suite 108, Washington, DC 20004. The record will close at 5:00 p.m. on Tuesday, February 11, 2014.
